AboutAbout
AI Engineer at Kroll, building production-grade Generative AI, Agentic AI, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for real enterprise use cases - not prototypes.

I work at the intersection of GenAI, data engineering, and software engineering, designing and deploying scalable, secure, and reliable AI systems embedded directly into business workflows.

My experience spans end-to-end AI development, including data ingestion, retrieval design, multi-agent orchestration, evaluation, and cloud-native deployment. I specialize in Agentic RAG systems for intelligent search, decision support, and workflow automation across large volumes of structured and unstructured data.

I place strong emphasis on retrieval quality, evaluation, and reliability, using metrics such as relevance, accuracy, MRR, hit rate, and groundedness to ensure production readiness and reduce hallucinations.

My work is primarily within the Azure ecosystem, leveraging Python, FastAPI, PySpark, Databricks, and modern GenAI frameworks to deliver AI solutions that are observable, secure, and cost-efficient.

What I do best:
• Enterprise GenAI & Agentic RAG architectures
• Multi-agent systems & retrieval optimization
• Production-grade AI APIs & cloud deployment
• AI evaluation, reliability & performance tuning

Always open to discussions on enterprise GenAI, Agentic RAG, and real-world AI systems.
